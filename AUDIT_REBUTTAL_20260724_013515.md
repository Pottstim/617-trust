## Net assessment

- **Real overall state is closer to 80/100 than 78.** GA4 (which the audit missed) and the JSON-LD depth (ProfessionalService + FAQPage + WebSite + founder E-E-A-T) are stronger than the audit credits.
- **Two true P0s the audit under-weighted:** (1) zero static headings on every page — this is the single biggest SEO defect and the audit ranked it correctly but didn't convey it's site-wide; (2) the n8n webhook returns 404, meaning the contact form is currently silently broken — the audit never tested the endpoint.
- **One stale claim to retract:** "strong testimonials / social proof." Testimonials were intentionally removed in the redesign.
- **One outright error:** "headings present on /services page." They are not — same SPA shell as every other route.
- **One already-done item:** GA4 is implemented and firing.

## Recommended next actions (merged with prior P0–P6 list)

1. **P0a** — Add server-rendered or static `<h1>`–`<h3>` to every page (pre-render the shell or inject heading HTML). Fixes the audit's #1 rec and the accessibility note simultaneously.
2. **P0b** — Repair `n8n.617east.com/webhook/617east-contact` (currently 404) and re-test end-to-end with a real submission.
3. **P1** — Wire founder photo onto About + JSON-LD `founder` (boosts E-E-A-T for GEO).
4. **P1** — Submit sitemap + request indexing on key pages in Google Search Console.
5. **P1** — Add a low-friction lead magnet CTA on the homepage.
6. **P2** — Build the blog/content engine (still missing).
7. **P3** — Tighten CSP to drop `unsafe-inline` where feasible.
